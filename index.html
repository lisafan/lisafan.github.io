I'm a software engineer at Google based in New York. Last year I received a Master's degree from Northeastern University studying Natural Language Processing under Professor Lu Wang. In 2017, I graduated from Tufts University with a BS/MS in Computer Science.

Currently, I'm working with the Funding Choices team to help publishers comply with privacy regulations and recover lost revenue from ad blockers.
